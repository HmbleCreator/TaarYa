The Gaia DR3 View of the Local Bubble and its Interaction with Nearby Molecular Clouds

Using 3D dust maps derived from Gaia DR3, we reconstruct the surface geometry of the Local Bubble. We identify three new star-forming regions on the bubble's surface triggered by the expansion of the supernovae-driven shell.

JWST observations of the core of the globular cluster 47 Tucanae

We present deep imaging of the core of 47 Tucanae with JWST/NIRCam. The data reveals a population of white dwarfs and cataclysmic variables previously hidden by crowding in Hubble Space Telescope images.
